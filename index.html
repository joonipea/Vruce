About Me

I’m a highly enthusiastic, resilient, and assertive activist with a passion for racial equity, economic justice, and reproductive justice. I excel in turning raw data and information into graphics and easy to consume social media posts. I’m adaptive and detail oriented individual who doesn’t shy away from a challenge.

I'm a black trans woman native to Durham, North Carolina. I've been a part of multiple organizations centered on LGBT rights, bodily autonomy, housing justice, and more. Community is extremely important to me, which is why work that includes keeping my community whole and healthy feels so natural.

Over the past three years I've worked with nonprofits to increase donor turnout as well as volunteer turnout through door-to-door canvasing, peer-to-peer fundraising, and digital outreach.

In my free time I produce and record music, paint, and take care of a house full of plants.
